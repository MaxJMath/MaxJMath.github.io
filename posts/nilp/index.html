Introduction and References

In this post I am going to try to explain the three step argument of Hopkins-Ravenel-Smith in their celebrated Nilpotence 1 paper. Step 3 in particular is easy to get lost in, and much of this post will be dedicated to emphasizing my personal take on which details one should focus on when reading this paper. At the end, I will briefly mention my own work on providing quantitative bounds on nilpotence orders via recursive estimates.
