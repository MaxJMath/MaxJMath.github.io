Introduction and References

In this post I am going to write about a pair of papers, BMS2 and HRW in order to explain the construction of certain filtrations on Topological Cyclic homology for quasisyntomic discrete rings (the BMS paper) and chromatically quasisyntomic commutative ring spectra (the HRW paper).

The goal of this note is to expand upon some of the constructions in both papers and hopefully provide a useful exposition in the process.
